0.2.0

Pokémon Voyager's second major release does a little bit of everything!

New Features
  • New Title Screen!
  • Skin Tone Customization - You can now customize your avatar's skin tone in addition to their outfit!
  • Fast travel! - Fly or warp to any city landmark using the Pokenav or any city map sign!
  • Last Used Ball - press L in battle to throw the last Poke Ball that you threw!
  • Rotation Battles! Battle with 3 Pokémon on the field at once, rotating between your best options! Available in the Battle Factory!
  • DexNav! - hunt your favorite Pokémon, chain dexnav searches for increased shiny chances, and more!
  • Upgraded PokeDex! - You have the ability to get a single PokeDex upgrade in the DexTech side quest
  • Pokémon Pinball! - Thanks to ShantyTown, you can play pinball games in the Andromeda Arcade in Andromeda Square for Casino Coins!
  • Achievement Menu - this has been redesigned to group achievements by category. More achievements have also been added!
  • Map Discovery! - you can track your Keplara region discovery for the Cartographer side quest!
New Content

Pokémon Voyager's story is complete through slightly after the third Antares League gym. Additionally, some aspects of the prologue have been streamlined to make it easier to decide where to go. The first two gyms have also been re-vamped.

New Art
  • New battle backgrounds have been added with images from Dio_FBI and carchagui, and some help from Jaizu!
  • Fakemon redesigns! - Arakid, Rakidone, Scuiret, Pteroret, and Ghoulslash have been fully redesigned thanks to Wednesday and Dio_FBI!
  • New UI colors! The bag, summary screen, trainer card, PokeNav, and DexNav have been given new designs and new colors to match the space theme of Voyager!
  • New trainer sprites! - Thanks to the awesome contributions by Kendy, we have some fancy trainer sprites!
Balance Adjustments
  • The Exp. All gives less experience to your team.

    You can customize the amount of experience awarded by Exp. All from the Difficulty Settings menu.
  • Tweaked levels, Pokémon, and movesets of several trainers.
  • Tweaked early learnset moves of several Pokémon to increase viability.
Difficulty Settings
  • You can choose between easier and harder gym puzzles.
  • Trainer AI can be configured from the Difficulty Settings menu.
Quality-of-Life Improvements
  • The Pokedex is opened when one of your Pokémon evolves into a new species.
  • The wild encounter menu remembers your most recent selection and sets the cursor to that for your next encounter.
  • The wild encounter menu can be turned off via the Game Settings menu.
  • Signposts are read automatically when you approach them. This can be disabled from the Game Settings menu.
  • You can now disable the start menu information window.
  • You now have the option of replacing a party member with a newly-caught Pokémon instead of it being sent directly to the PC. Hold items of replaced party members will be returned to your bag.
  • You can now amass up to 50000 coins!
  • The Casino Pokémon Prize Menu includes a shiny toggling option for 5000 coins!
  • A TM Case has been added with party icons to show TM compatability!
  • Change your name and gender at the Andromeda Villa City Hall!
Randomizer Updates
  • Chaos Mode has been added. When enabled, random values are generated on each instance at run-time instead of shuffling values around!
  • The randomizer/challenge options no longer try to force the original starters on the player as often
Bugs fixed
  • Difficulty data scaling was not applied properly at lower difficulties; now it is.
  • Battle experience should be awarded in a consistent order: Pokémon who participated in the battle, Pokémon holding Exp. Share, and then the rest of your party if Exp. All is on.
  • The type effectiveness randomizer does not cause most moves to be ineffective.
  • The nuzlocke mode + whiteout behavior edge cases have been handled to prevent exploring the game with an empty/dead party
