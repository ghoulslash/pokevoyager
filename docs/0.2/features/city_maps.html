City Maps

Several of Keplara's cities are quite large with lots of interesting places to visit. To help you decide where to go, each city has a detailed map about where the interesting places are!

You can access the city maps from the Pokenav, or from one of the many blue signs in each city.

When the map opens up, move the cursor around to see all the different city landmarks.
Adjacent maps are labeled as well.
In addition to being a handy guide, you can use the city maps to instantly travel to a destination landmark!
Fast travel is typically unlocked for a city once you've completed the main story events there. You can fast travel from within your current city, or from the Fly map, or from the Pokenav!
