Gym badge obtained, we're not quite done with Sirius Wharf yet. Remember that tech expo your rival mentioned? The queue has cleared away. Go check it out!
Is it even possible to attend a public event without Team Nova showing up? We'll never know. But this time, the stakes are real and the firepower is intense. Your odds of success here are... low. It's probably best to cut your losses and yield the battle.
You'll be imprisoned in the auditorium with the other expo attendees, held in place by a few guards. Someone named Triton is here with you. He laid low, so his apparent Pokemon expertise went un-noticed. Seems like a pretty big oversight. It's certainly going to lead to an opportunity for escape.

The unprepared grunts dealt with, Triton has a plan for what to do. Sneak over to the train station to go recruit some outside help.
It's a mixed success. Team Nova manages to cut power off to the train before you can get anywhere too useful. Now you're stranded in Tvashtar Summit , with no help on the way to Sirius Wharf . Better look for an exit. Make your way up to the summit and eventually you'll find a hermit who lives up here. He is sympathetic and will both send you back to Sirius Wharf , and send word of the assault to Andromeda City.
When you arrive, Triton and the Nova admin, Hyperion, are battling. She sees you arrive and decides to consolidate Nova's position in the city. Time to team up with Triton and disrupt that notion as much as you can while waiting for reinforcements! He will show you his party so you know what to expect.

During this tag battle, Hyperion and Triton will only attack each other. Ignoring the Nova recruit might be tempting, but he'll play a supporting role setting up screens and using Heal Pulse if you don't disrupt him. You won't have much of a chance against Hyperion if she takes out Triton. Set up status if you can, or go after the recruit if you can't. When you're ready to leave, head through the gatehouse to Ascraeus Mons , the major mountain standing between you and Point Vega . It's a long trek, so bring supplies!
As you approach the exit to the mountain, you'll encounter this paranoid fellow. After he realizes you don't pose any threat to him, he'll teach you a cool new technique!
You can use Psychic Terrain in the overworld to dodge attacks from wild Pokemon. Check out the move's page for details!
Now you've left Ascraeus Mons . You're not quite to Point Vega yet. The last stretch, Tharsis Terra , has a few items waiting for you if you're patient to explore.
Head due south to Point Vega , or if you're in the mood for some story, you can head west first. It's available to you now, but we'll cover what happens over there in the next chapter. Before heading into the gym, it's a good idea to collect the Utility Umbrella .
Finally, time to collect your fourth gym badge! The Pokemon in this gym don't adhere to a specific type, but a specific place - they are all at home on the beach! The only required trainer in this gym should be the leader, Magaclite.
