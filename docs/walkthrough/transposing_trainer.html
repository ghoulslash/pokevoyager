Transposing Trainer

This optional side quest offers you an additional rival who will periodically ask you for an inverse battle. The illusionist Rarai has come to find regular Pokemon battles a bit stale, and wants something exciting to tweak the rules a bit. Indulge her and she'll thank you with useful items or advice!

You'll meet Rarai for the first time in Versant Megrez, the mountainous area to the west of Sol City.
